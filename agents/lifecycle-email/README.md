# Lifecycle & Email

Your Lifecycle & Email hire for a solo founder stack. I draft welcome
sequences, write your newsletter, design activation and re-engagement
drips, craft churn-save emails, and plan product announcements —
email plus the matching in-app copy. I never send. You approve and
trigger from your own platform.

## First prompts

- "Draft a 5-email welcome series for new signups"
- "Draft this week's newsletter — here's what shipped"
- "Design a re-activation drip for users who signed up but never activated"
- "Draft a save email for accounts that downgraded"
- "Plan the email + in-app announcement for {feature}"

## Skills

- `onboard-me` — 3-question setup: email platform, voice, product
  milestones (signup → activation → aha → habit → upgrade).
- `draft-welcome-sequence` — 5-email series (Day 0/1/3/7/14 default).
- `write-newsletter` — single-edition newsletter with one through-line.
- `design-lifecycle-campaign` — event-triggered, branching drips.
- `draft-churn-save` — cancel-flow save with a genuine option.
- `plan-product-announcement` — launch email + matching in-app copy.

## Cross-agent reads

Reads `../head-of-marketing/product-marketing-context.md` before any
substantive output. If missing, stops and asks the founder to run the
Head of Marketing's `define-positioning` first. Optionally reads
`../head-of-marketing/launches/` and `../seo-content/outputs.json`
when relevant — both are graceful on miss.

## Outputs

All outputs land as markdown under a topic subfolder (`sequences/`,
`newsletters/`, `drips/`, `saves/`, `announcements/`) plus a record
in `outputs.json` (shown in the Overview dashboard).

## Source-template note

Gumloop's featured email catalog skews support and SDR; lifecycle is
almost entirely coverage-gap territory. Beehiiv is the only direct
source template, covering newsletters. The other four skills are
built from role knowledge, not a template.
